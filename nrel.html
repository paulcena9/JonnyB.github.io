NREL Software Development Internship

Year-long SULI undergraduate internship (June 2023 - May 2024) with the Data, Analysis & Visualization group at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory

About NOODLES

NOODLES is a cross-platform/device/tool protocol for collaborative visualization.

NOODLES was developed at the National Renewable Energy Laboratory (NREL), as a capability of the Insight Center.

The Problem: Collaborative Visualization

Data visualization is difficult to do, and researchers often invest large amounts of time developing a skillset using one or two tools. Collaboration between researchers is difficult because there is no centralized platform where a wide variety of processed data can exist in the same 3D scene.

The Solution: NOODLES

NREL Object Orientated Data layout and exploration system. Most of my work involved creating a filter for NOODLES to allow the exports of VTK/Paraview software to be introduced in a Noodles Scene. Orzo-OpenAI leveraged the existing Orzo, Noodles Rendering Client built in Python, and incorporated the OpenAI API into the client, allowing users to invoke visualizations with natural language and custom parameters.
